Data Collection & Usage Notice Please read before continuing

Ctrl Value collects and processes the following information to provide its financial tracking services.

What we collect

  • Account information: your name, email address, and account credentials
  • Financial data: account balances, transactions, positions, valuations, and budgets that you import or enter manually
  • Usage data: login timestamps, session activity, and feature interactions

How we use it

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Ctrl Value service
  • To send security notifications such as email verification codes and password reset links
  • To support multi-user workspace collaboration where you have granted access to other users

Who we share it with

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • Your data may be processed by our infrastructure providers (Microsoft Azure) under data processing agreements
  • Your data may be disclosed where required by applicable law

How long we keep it

  • Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active
  • You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting support

Your rights

  • You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data
  • You may withdraw your consent at any time by deactivating your account
  • For privacy enquiries, refer to the repository documentation
